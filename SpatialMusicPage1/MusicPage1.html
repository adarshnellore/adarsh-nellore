Spatial Audio

We often listen to music to alter how we perceive our surroundings. More specifically, spatial elements within music affect how we experience the physical spaces we’re in and the stochastic events that occur within that space. Emphasizing this relationship can make the experience of listening to music more immersive.

What you’re about to experience is an interface that emphasizes the relationship between physical space, music production, and listening in which producers create AI-generated music by designing a physical space and the stochastic events within, which the listener then has the freedom to experience on their own spatial terms..
